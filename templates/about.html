About Us

EmoGrow is a web-based app designed to promote Social-Emotional Learning (SEL) through personalized emotional check-ins, interactive lessons, and gamified activities. It makes SEL accessible, engaging, and measurable for students and individuals alike

    Main Fetures

  • Tracks emotional states and fosters self-awareness
  • Teaches skills like empathy, emotional regulation, and conflict resolution.
  • Gamified Activities: Encourages engagement through challenges and rewards.
  • Progress Tracking: Helps users and educators monitor SEL growth over time.
  • Teacher and Parent Integration: Bridges the gap between home and school for holistic SEL development.
